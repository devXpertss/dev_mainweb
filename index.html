Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the purity of gold jewelry?

All our gold jewelry comes with proper hallmark certification from BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards). Each piece is tested and certified for purity. We provide certificates with every purchase guaranteeing the gold purity.

Do you offer custom jewelry design?

Yes! We specialize in custom jewelry design. Our expert craftsmen can create unique pieces based on your preferences. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss your requirements and get a personalized quote.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unused jewelry in original condition. For exchanges, we accept old gold jewelry and provide fair market value. All returns must include original certificates and packaging.

How are the prices calculated?

Our prices are based on live gold/silver rates updated daily. The total cost includes: Metal Cost (weight √ó current rate) + Making Charges (15% of metal cost). All prices are transparent with detailed breakdown provided.

Do you provide home delivery?

Yes, we provide secure home delivery for all orders. Delivery charges may apply based on location. For high-value items, we use insured courier services. Contact us for delivery options in your area.
