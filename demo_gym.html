Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Find the information you need about our gym, memberships, and services right here.

What are your operating hours?

We are open 24/7 for all members, giving you the flexibility to work out whenever it suits your schedule. Our staffed hours are from 8 AM to 8 PM on weekdays and 10 AM to 5 PM on weekends for assistance and inquiries.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 3-day free trial for all new visitors. This allows you to explore our facilities, try out some of our equipment, and even join a class to see if we're the right fit for you. Just stop by the front desk during staffed hours to get started.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Our memberships are on a convenient month-to-month basis with no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time with a 30-day notice, giving you complete control over your membership.

Is personal training included in my membership?

Personal training is an additional service, though our Ignite Pro and Elite membership plans include a few complimentary sessions to help you get started with a certified trainer. We also offer discounted rates for members who wish to purchase additional sessions.
